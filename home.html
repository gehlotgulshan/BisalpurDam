Summary Of Bisalpur Dam

Who built the Bisalpur Dam?

Bisalpur Dam was constructed in 1999 when the Congress was in power in Rajasthan and in 2004, the BJP-led government started laying pipelines to connect the city with Bisalpur. Finally, in 2009, water from Bisalpur started coming to Jaipur.

What is the history of Bisalpur Dam?

Bisalpur Dam is a gravity dam on the Banas River near Bisalpur in Tonk district, Rajasthan, India. The dam was completed in 1999 for the purpose of irrigation and water supply.

What is Bisalpur famous for?

Bisalpur is famous for Annual Ramleela Fest, Dubey Taalab, Guleshwarnath Temple, Dargah Hazrat Gazi kamaal Degree college and Nagaich Market.
