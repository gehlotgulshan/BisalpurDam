Visit This Dam

Bisalpur Dam Project is being supplied to different places today and the farmers are being benefited through the canals. Mainly Jaipur, Dausa, Tonk and Sawai Madhopur districts are taking advantage of this Dam Water. There are crowds of people coming to visit all over the Rajasthan and country.

How to Reach Bisalpur Dam

Airway

The nearest airport to the Bisalpur Dam is Jaipur International Airport which is 156 kilometers.

Railway

The Nearest Railway station to the Bisalpur Dam is Bundi Railway Station which is 85 Kilometers.

Road

The Bisalpur Dam is by Road 164 Kilometers from Jaipur and 72 Kilometers from Tonk Districts.

DIRECTIONS

Bisalpur Drinking water cum irrigation project is constructed across river Banas, a tributary of river Chambal near village Bisalpur of Deoli tehsil in Tonk district of Rajasthan. The dam is about 25 km from Deoli town on Jaipur Kota road.The dam was completed in 1999 for the purpose of irrigation and water supply
